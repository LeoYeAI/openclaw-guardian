## 🎯 当前第一个Pitch：Google Remy

### 背景
- Hugh Langley (BI) 5/5独家：Google内部测试"Remy"，24/7个人Agent
- 直接对标OpenClaw生态
- 目前报道只有Google视角，缺少"已有玩家"的回应

### Pitch角度
**"Google validates the market — but here's what Remy won't give you"**

核心论点：
1. Google进场 = 验证了24/7 AI Agent是真需求（不是玩具）
2. 但Remy = Google读你所有数据（Gmail, Calendar, Drive）
3. MyClaw = 同样的能力，但Agent跑在用户自己的服务器上
4. 数据：18,000+付费实例，$100K/天收入，证明市场已存在

### 给记者的评论（Draft）
> "Google entering the personal AI agent space with Remy validates what we've been seeing for months — there's massive demand for always-on AI assistants. We have over 18,000 paying users running their own AI agents 24/7. The key difference is where the agent lives. With Remy, Google holds your data. With MyClaw, your agent runs on your own server — same capability, full ownership."
> — Leo Ye, Founder & CEO, MyClaw.ai
